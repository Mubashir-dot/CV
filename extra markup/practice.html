ABOUT US

Imtiaz Super Market is the pioneer in the retail industry of Pakistan,
providing an ultimate shopping experience to its consumers.

Imtiaz Super Market is accessible in various urban areas of Pakistan to give an amazing and cost-effective shopping experience with incentives for cash shopping background for the customer base we serve.
